Adaptations

  1. Leaves are reduced to spines to prevent loss of water from the surface of leaves.Stomata are less in number and sunken in the surface.
  2. The surface of leaves become shiny and glazed to reflect light and heat.
  3. Both leaves and stem have a thick waxy coatingto prevent loss of water in hot weather.
  4. Mechanical tissues and conducting tissues are well deeloped.
  5. Stem becomes green to take up function of leaves.Most plants such as Cacti have stems becomes fleshy to store water.
  6. They have a well developed root system that penetrates deep inside the soil in search of water.
